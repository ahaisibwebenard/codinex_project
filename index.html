About codinex

Founded in Mbarara, built by engineers

CODINEX Computers Ltd was founded in Mbarara in 2012 by IT professionals and computer engineers, with a practical approach to customer service. We manage large-scale IT projects while keeping a close eye on day-to-day service and maintenance, connecting with thousands of customers a year across networks and security, software development, and hardware repair and maintenance in East Africa.

Our vision

To become Uganda's ICT centre of choice by committing to world-class technology.

Our mission

To sustain integrity, professionalism, customer satisfaction, and computer and business ethics as the foundation of our relationships with stakeholders, customers, suppliers, employees, shareholders and the community.

Our core values

Commitment to excellence, diversity, innovation, integrity and teamwork.

Our objectives

Vendor-certified, internationally recognised IT education — advanced, corporate and customised to the client.
